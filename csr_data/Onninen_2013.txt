group events 2012
 onninen groups profitability improved considerably in 2012 the positive development was enabled by the clear
		 improvement of the profitability of onninens business operations in finland

 onninen oy acquired the share capital of lämpötukku oy from panostaja oyj in spring 2012 the acquisition
		 improved further onninens strong competence in the wholesale of heating plumbing and ventilation products
		 in the finnish markets

 conny dyrenäs started his work as the managing director of onninen sweden in august 2012

 in 2012 onninen russia continued to increase its already strong project business operations and for example
		 signed an agreement with the olympic winter games in sochi to provide a snowmaking system for the 2014 winter olympics

 moreover onninen russia started deliveries for the major project in the st petersburg waterworks
		 vodokanal in the latter part of 2012

 the sap system rollout project continued as planned and onninen russia joined the groups joint enterprise
		 resource planning erp system in the beginning of january 2013
