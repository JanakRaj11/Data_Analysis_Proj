                                          mges blount generating station home peregrine falcons are an
                                          endangered species in wisconsin due to pesticide use that began
                                          in the 1940s manmade nest boxes at power plants have proven
                                          to be ideal homes for nesting falcons more than half the falcons
                                          hatched each year in the state come from power plant nests

      frightful intrudy laid four eggs in the spring all four chicks successfully hatched and fledged mges
