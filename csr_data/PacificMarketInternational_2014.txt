2014 corporate responsibility report

with this 5th corporate responsibility
report pmi demonstrates a commitment
to sustainability that has matured to build
value for the company its manufacturing
partners and customers a decade ago pmi
embraced the frameworks and opportunities
of sustainability building upon the strengths
of its legacy brands pmi created products
that exemplify outstanding design matched
with an ethic of responsibility to customers
communities and the environment
pmi has deepened its engagement with
factories building strong employee loyalty
and awardwinning environmental manage
ment programs this comprehensive
approach has placed pmi at the forefront
of the household product industry
