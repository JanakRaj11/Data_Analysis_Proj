highlight our commitment in promoting economic environmental and social sustainability

management in hong kong china and singapore deliver sustainability initiatives and reports

the global reporting initiative gri g4 sustainability reporting guidelines

governance which is available in our annual report can be accessed on our corporate
website wwwsinocom

     message from
     the sustainability committee
     we have been reporting on corporate sustainability performance to our stakeholders
     since 2011 and have fully integrated environmental and social factors into our business
     decisions our sustainability programmes are built around four csr pillars  sino green
     sino care sino art and sino heritage while maintaining our efforts on ongoing
     sustainable programmes we have also increased our attention on innovative green
     programmes and initiatives

     in december 2015 government representatives of more than 200 countries signed the
     paris agreement which called for closer cooperation between governments businesses
     and communities to keep global temperature increase to below 2 degrees celsius from
     current temperatures and to stabilise carbon emissions by the end of the 21st century we
     have committed ourselves to be a part of the paris agreement by establishing a baseline for
     our carbon emission and identifying key areas for improvement through carbon auditing
     we also take part in the hksar environmental protection departments carbon disclosure
     initiative and our carbon emission reductions are reported on an annual basis

     after meeting and exceeding our 2015 carbon reduction target which was to reduce
     emissions from the 2008 level by 15 we began a new chapter in tackling climate change
     by setting a revised target of 16 carbon emissions reduction by 2020 based on the 2012
     emission level1 this goal will be accomplished through our green initiatives and green
     property management as well as our keen support of the governments energy saving
     schemes such as energy saving charter on indoor temperature learn more about our
     environmental initiatives in creating green environment

     we believe that with love and care every child can blossom sino care has been working
     closely with different organisations on a wide spectrum of programmes from the sino
     children mentorship programme to the go code project and other care and charity
     projects to nurture our future generation since its launch in 2014 the go code project has
     empowered more than 320 children from underprivileged families through coding courses
     in addition to learning coding tools and knowledge students can also benefit from the
     training in logic and problemsolving through trial and error as well as fostering the right
     attitude in using information technology we are also pleased to report that the sino children
     mentorship programme has extended its service to kwun tong to benefit the young children
     in the district learn more about our social care programmes in caring our community

     sino art continues to make arts and culture part of our community through our sino art
     in community in collaboration with local artists we have completed two projects during
     the reporting period to create murals and organise workshop for young people more
     details about sino art could be found in building a beautiful community in addition in
     recognition of sino heritages efforts towards heritage preservation the former fullerton
     building in singapore which houses the fullerton hotel was gazetted on 7 december 2015
     as singapores 71st national monument by the national heritage board learn more about
     heritage preservation in promoting heritage conservation

     we shall continue to promote sustainability through caring the environment and the society
     we look forward to working more closely with our stakeholders for a sustainable roadmap
     to build a better future

     corporate governance
     企業管治
     we maintain a structure of corporate governance which ensures that we conduct our business
     with sound governance business ethics and corporate integrity in order to safeguard the
     interests of our shareholders and other stakeholders at each of our listed companies the
     board of directors the board is the highest governance body which assumes overall
     leadership and ensures implementation and maintenance of effective internal controls
     to protect the interests of the company and its shareholders the board is composed of
     executive directors nonexecutive directors and independent nonexecutive directors who
     all contribute valuable views for the boards deliberation and subsequent decision making

     to enhance the function of the board four board committees namely audit committee
     remuneration committee nomination committee and compliance committee have been
     set up to take up different responsibilities each board committee is chaired by a director
     with other board members as committee members while the compliance committee also
     comprises relevant department heads and the compliance officer clear information on the
     performance of our group is provided through timely publication of interim and annual
     results and reports circulars to shareholders and announcements in accordance with the
     requirements of the listing rules

     we take an active role in managing business risks through internal control and risk
     management framework each business unit or department is required to identify assess
     evaluate and mitigate risks twice a year according to their potential impact and the likelihood
     of occurrence we adopt a precautionary approach to risk management in operational
     planning and development we disclose our risk management structure and response
     strategy to stakeholders on a regular basis through our annual reports

     all management and staff must comply with the code of conduct the code which sets
     out our position and standards regarding operating ethics and employee conduct including
     bribery prevention and avoidance of conflict of interest any breaches of the code are
     handled in strict accordance with internal disciplinary procedures we invite experts from
     external organisations such as the independent commission against corruption the equal
     opportunities commission and the privacy commissioner for personal data periodically to
     deliver trainings to our employees
