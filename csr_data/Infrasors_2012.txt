                                                                      the continuing global economic crisis and domestic regulatory constraints have created a challenging economic environment for the

                                                                      impacted our sales margins


                                                                      the south african legislative environment remains challenging to navigate as it grows more onerous the time and costs involved in
                                                                      obtaining the appropriate governmental and regulatory approvals are becoming more for the mining industry and no guarantees are given
