  seed konara quercus serrata
   this years cover continues on last years theme of seeds as a
   symbol of a recyclebased society the illustration is of an
   acorn from the konara quercus serrata tree the konara is a
   deciduous tree of the fagaceae beech family and grows in
   forests throughout japan since ancient times the konara has
   been vital to humans used as charcoal for growing shiitake
   mushrooms to make furniture and to produce starch for
