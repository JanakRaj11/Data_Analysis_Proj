developing talent
our talent more specifically the talent of our 36000 employees around the
world is what drives our success as a company we are committed to providing
meaningful opportunities for our people to advance their careers within textron
helping them succeed and driving growth for our company when our people
advance it creates exciting opportunities for others to take on new roles and
allows us to bring in new talent to join us and begin their career journey


we look at our talent strategy as an active pipeline in which we continually create
exciting opportunities for our people to grow and contribute in meaningful ways
that in turn opens additional opportunities for others


it begins by matching the right people with the right positions ongoing
discussions enable us to communicate and set performance expectations and then
reward outstanding resultsoriented performance


through our annual talent review process our senior leaders assess the talent
needs of each business ensuring that top talent has challenging assignments
as the businesses grow and evolve this process along with ongoing workforce
planning provides insights into the current and future talent needs of the
organization


we build the capabilities of our people through informal and formal learning
experiences with opportunities to learn advanced technical skills as well as skills
for their ongoing professional development textron university offers courses that
are both specific to their business as well as their function
