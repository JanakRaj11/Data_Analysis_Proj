                             contains up to 25 post consumer recycled polystyrene and was developed for use in foodservice applications
                             polyrenew polystyrene offers a durable and more ecofriendly commercial foodservice packaging alternative for
                             items such as takeout clamshells bowls plates cups egg cartons meat trays and other foam food packaging
